About

Congratulations on receiving your Software Engineer Interview with Bloomberg! Our engineers have a passion for writing reusable, adequate solutions to complex problems. Here at Bloomberg, we keep an open mind and adapt to an ever changing market landscape. Our teams develop software that impact thousands of user globally.

Things to remember:

  • You may code in the programming language that you are most comfortable with
  • Coding portion can be completed on your choice of whiteboard, paper or laptop
  • Software engineers get to choose which department they can work at a career fair
